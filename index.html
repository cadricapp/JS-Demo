Meaning of Design Thinking

I am choosing to talk about Design Thinking in this project because I have a deep interest and passion for how user stories are mapped out and created into tasks for software developers and designers to create beautiful applications. Design thinking is used in all kinds of businesses to help get your ideas out of your head and on paper. Design thinking workshops are conducted with multiple people involved in the business of interest. These individuals are called stakeholders. Stakeholders consist of product users, product buyers, product developers, and business owners, to name a few.
